Projects and Experience

SlackOverFlow

A forum for GA students to post questions and receive answers in a interactive atmosphere. Integrated third party API’s for user authorization and user response capabilities. This web app has the ability to update dynamically while giving a visual indication to questions posted in the last 30 minutes. Main features include; Data Base Organization, User Validation, Question Log with time stamp updated dynamically, Ease for User to respond to a question.

Lowe’s Digital Challenge

With a team of fellow GA alumni, I participated in a weekend Digital challenge sponsored by Lowe’s. Teams were given the task to design a web app targeted for the mobile market to empower home owners and allow them to take pride in the home. My team and I decided to create a game for young children to young to help out with DIY projects. We made a simple 2D building simulator that kids will have fun playing in order for parents to involve the whole family.
